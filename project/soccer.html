Soccer: The Beautiful Game

Discover the history, legends, and latest updates in soccer

History of Soccer

Soccer, also known as football, is the world's most popular sport. It originated in England in the 19th century but traces its roots back to ancient China, Greece, and Rome, where similar games were played. The modern rules of soccer were standardized by the Football Association in 1863.

The FIFA World Cup

The FIFA World Cup is the most prestigious soccer tournament, held every four years. The first tournament was held in 1930, and it has since become the most-watched sporting event in the world. Countries from all continents compete for the ultimate prize in soccer.
